About the Program

Shaping the Teachers of Tomorrow

Our Teacher Training Program is built on a foundation of evidence-based pedagogy, mentorship, and real-world classroom experience.

Fully Accredited National Board Certified

EduForward's Teacher Training Program has been cultivating outstanding educators since 2012. Our multidisciplinary curriculum integrates pedagogical theory, classroom technology, and student psychology to produce teachers who are truly equipped for modern classrooms.

Key Objectives

  • Develop subject mastery and pedagogy skills
  • Build inclusive classroom strategies
  • Master digital tools and ed-tech integration
  • Cultivate leadership and reflective practice

Program Highlights

  • 6-month intensive structured training
  • 200+ hours of live classroom practicals
  • 1-on-1 mentorship with industry experts
  • Nationally recognized certification
Course Curriculum

A Structured Path to Mastery

Our program is divided into progressive modules that build upon each other, taking you from foundations to advanced teaching excellence.

  • History and Philosophy of Education
  • Models of Teaching and Learning
  • Bloom's Taxonomy & Learning Objectives
  • Child Psychology and Development Stages
  • Classroom Environment Design
  • Designing Standards-Aligned Curricula
  • Lesson Plan Frameworks (5E, Madeline Hunter)
  • Differentiated Instruction Strategies
  • Assessment Design: Formative vs Summative
  • Creating Inclusive Learning Materials
  • Positive Behavior Reinforcement Techniques
  • Conflict Resolution and De-escalation
  • Motivational Psychology for Students
  • Building a Respectful Classroom Culture
  • Working with Diverse Learning Needs
  • Google Workspace for Education
  • Learning Management Systems (Moodle, Canvas)
  • Gamification Tools: Kahoot, Quizizz, Padlet
  • Flipped Classroom Implementation
  • Accessibility in Digital Learning
  • Supervised Teaching Practicals in Partner Schools
  • Peer Observation and Feedback Sessions
  • Portfolio Development and Reflection
  • Final Teaching Demonstration
  • Capstone Curriculum Design Project
